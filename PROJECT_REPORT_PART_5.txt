Potential Questions & Answers:
Q: Why MERN stack?
A: Popular, JavaScript everywhere, large community, fast development

Q: Why Redux Toolkit?
A: Simplified Redux setup, built-in best practices, great developer experience

Q: How do you handle authentication?
A: JWT tokens stored in httpOnly cookies, middleware protection on routes

Q: What about payment processing?
A: Currently Cash on Delivery, easy to add Stripe in future

Q: What about security?
A: Password hashing, JWT auth, protected routes, input validation, more can be added

Q: Mobile support?
A: Fully responsive, works on all devices, could create native app with React Native
